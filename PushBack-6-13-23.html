Motor Morsels: VInFast, Nio, NACS Fo’ Real Tho

VinFast , the Vietnamese automaker, is accelerating the EV transition in North America, delivering nearly 2,000 VF 8 vehicles with battery ranges of up to 264 miles. Certified by the Environmental Protection Agency, the EVs are priced "competitively" from $46K in the U.S. and $53,600 in Canada. They come standard with a 10-year warranty, an Advanced Driver Assistance System, and the ability for over-the-air updates.
 

Giphy/Warner Bros.

The price war, which is more of a discount skirmish, has China's EV maker, Nio , cutting prices on its cars and delaying research and expansion spending. The company's margins fell from 14.6% a year ago to 3.9% in Q4 2022 before crash landing at 1.5% in Q1 2023. As of March, the company has $2.07B to work with, 1/22nd the price of Twitter for scale.
 

Ford and GM don't always agree, but when they do, the industry changes. Since both automakers have adopted Tesla's North American Charging Standard , other companies are moving to adopt the hardware for their networks. Quebec-based Flo, US-based Blink, and ChargePoint have already announced plans to adopt the standard.

Telling it like it is — VinFast is delivering its best yet, NIO is losing gains, and Tesla’s North American charging standard really is becoming the standard.

 
CULTURE

Young Talent in the Spider-Verse

Giphy

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse has been delighting audiences worldwide, snatching up more than $208M in its opening weekend alone. Amidst the accolades and applause, a remarkable secret was uncovered — one scene in the film wasn't crafted by seasoned Hollywood artists but rather by a talented 14-year-old from Toronto.

In January, Preston Mutanga, an avid LEGO enthusiast, decided to recreate the movie trailer entirely out of colorful plastic bricks. 🧱

Sony Pictures

His impressive video caught the attention of Phil Lord and Christopher Miller (the brilliant minds behind The Lego Movie and Spider-Verse). Impressed with the sophistication of Preston's creation, the duo decided to weave his LEGO magic into the film. 🎬

Inspired by the inspired. Lord said of Preston’s creative video, “The idea in Spider-Verse is that a hero can come from anywhere. And here comes this heroic young person who’s inspired by the movie that was inspired by people like him.”

Telling it like it is — You’re never too young (or, too old) to chase your dreams. 💭
