Tanks-Giving with SVG Motors
 
 
SVG Motors is celebrating Tanks-Giving!
 
The SVG team will fill tanks in their communities from the 15th to the 22nd. People will go out daily to scour the local gas stations for cars with SVG logos and pay for their gas.
 
Gas prices, holiday travel, and a dedication to customers long after the day of their purchase led the dealer group to give back in this way.
 
Telling it like it is -  Some November giveaways are turkey, and some help you get to the turkey. Both are great!

Used Car Value Oddities
 
 
Data shows that used car prices are easing, but some older cars maintain their value. Allow us to tell you which models have depreciated the least in the last 5 years and why we think that is (without researching at all).
  • -7.30% - Jeep Wrangler - "Jeep owners" is the only explanation we can offer. 
  • -8.70% - Jeep Wrangler Unlimited - See above
  • -14.6% - Porsche 911 - Porsche is one of those brands that just get cooler when they're old.
  • -14.9% - Toyota Tacoma - Ford people bash Chevy, Chevy people bash Ford. Nobody bashes Toyota Tacomas. 
  • -16.3% - Honda Civic - Nobody knows how old Honda Civics are because their drivers are always the same age. 
  • -18.2% - Subaru BRZ - Go look this car up in Google images and tell us you think it should be removed from the list. We'll wait. 
  • -19.4% - Ford Mustang - We gotta blame the movie "Gone in 60 Seconds" for this one. 
  • -19.8% - Toyota Corolla - We asked, and no Corolla driver has ever driven faster than the speed limit. That's why they last so long. 
  • -19.9% - Nissan Versa - We forgot this car exists, and so did the aging power of time. 
  • -20.2% - Chevrolet Camaro - Apparently, Camaros are pretty popular and well-made. 

Telling it like it is - Feel free to pass our notes to your used car manager.

2030, Air EV, and Bye Bye Blue Checks?
 
Renault has a new EV and software-oriented standalone unit. Ampere will develop, manufacture, and sell as a full-fledged brand under Renault. The company will employ about 10K people and has a six subcompact and compact EV lineup planned before 2030.
 
Someday, historians will try to explain the automotive significance of 2030. We can only wish them luck because we don't get it either.
 
 
Ford has an interesting new first to report. Bigger electric trucks can power houses or let other EVs borrow juice in a pinch. Now three F-150 Lightning owners are assisting the first-ever sporting event flyover by an electric plane. The plane will travel from Hartford, CT, to Easton, PA, stopping three times to connect to trucks for charging.
 
We love to see innovation! From the ground. Where it is safe. (Seriously, Would you want to get on that plane?)

Reddit Adds Muting
 
 
Rejoice! Reddit may be useful again!
 
🤫🔇 In a post on r/reddit, the platform announced it will allow user to mute subreddits.
 
🤖🍎 The feature has started rolling out with Android and iOS now and will come to the website version later.
 
🙈🙉 Muted subreddits will not appear in your notifications, recommendations, or when filtering new content in the "Popular " view.
 
⏳🔭 Eventually, muting will apply to the "All" and "Discover" views also.
 
Telling it like it is - Goodbye forever r/MildlyInteresting, you were boring the entire time.
