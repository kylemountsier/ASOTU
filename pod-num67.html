Musk Tweets…Dogecoin Jumps
 
 

Musk Tweeted Jan 14th that, after trying the cryptocurrency out for a month, Tesla will begin accepting the meme-based Dogecoin for purchases of merchandise. As a vocal supporter of the cryptocurrency, Musk has been influential in its penetration of corporate culture and its explosive increase in value over 2021. With other auto giants preparing to launch their EVs this year, crypto-based meme marketing may become something we see more of in the near future.
