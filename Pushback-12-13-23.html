National Moves

Labor Tensions Rise

  • Union-Busting Allegations:   Workers  at Honda, Hyundai, and VW accuse management of unfair labor practices.

  • Organizing Rights Upheld:  Despite challenges, unionization efforts persevere under federally protected rights.

Legislative Update

  • CARS Act Passed:  The US House  opposes  Biden's EV mandate, with the bill now heading to the Senate.

  • Implications for EPA:  The Act could restrict EPA from future emissions regulations.

EV Infrastructure Developments

  • First Biden-Funded Charger:  A significant step for EV adoption, the first government-funded charger  opened  in Ohio.

  • Slow and Steady:  Despite a slow pace, 26 states have begun deploying chargers under the NEVI program.

What's the Biggest Challenge for the Auto Industry in 2024?
  • 🤝 Labor Struggles: Labor disputes and unionization efforts
  • 🚗 Policy Shifts: Legislative changes impacting mobility
  • 🔌 Charging Infrastructure: Slow rollout of EV infrastructure
  • 💰 Economic Pressures: Rising costs and interest rates affecting sales and production
  • 🌍 Environmental Concerns: Balancing industry growth with sustainability
  • 🤔 Other Challenges: Have different thoughts? Let us know!

 

Investments and Changes

Renault's Nissan Stake Reduction

  • Downsizing Holdings:  Renault plans to  reduce  its stake in Nissan, aiming to lower it to 15% from 43%.

  • Strategic Overhaul:  This move is part of Renault's broader business strategy, including an IPO for its EV unit Ampere.

Honda's Strategic Shifts

  • E Minicar Discontinued in Europe:  Honda  ceases  sales of its electric E Minicar, falling short of its sales targets.

  • Future Focus:  Shifting gears, Honda now eyes new EV models like the e:Ny1 SUV.

GM's Hydrogen Innovation

  • Hydrogen-Powered Trucks:  GM  collaborates  with Komatsu, using Hydrotec fuel cells in heavy-duty mining trucks.

  • Environmental Impact:  A green initiative, these trucks will only emit water vapor.

Ford's Production Pivot

  • F-150 Lightning Production Cut:  Ford  halves  its 2024 production plans for the electric F-150 Lightning.

  • Market Responsiveness:  Adjustments made in response to the current market demand and sales trends.

New and Coming Soon

Corvette E-Ray's Exciting Launch

  • GM Unveils Hybrid Corvette:  The Corvette E-Ray  debuts , becoming the quickest Corvette model yet.

  • Pricing Announced:  Starting at $104,900 for the coupe version.

Volvo and ChargePoint's Joint Venture

  • Charging Network Expansion:  Volvo and ChargePoint  establish  50 new DC fast chargers at Starbucks locations.

  • Convenient Road-Tripping:  These chargers, placed along a 1,350-mile route, facilitate long-distance EV travel.

Nissan Note's Refresh

  • Mid-Cycle Update:  The Nissan  Note  gets a facelift with a new front fascia and unique interior features like a swivel passenger seat.

  • Hybrid Powertrain:  Available in both FWD and AWD, starting at around $15,800.

A Car Full of Joy

Bucks County Courier Times / Daniella Heminghaus

Davis Acura in Langhorne has teamed up with the Toys for Tots program to help collect toys for families struggling to bring presents home for their children during the holidays. 🎁

As part of their efforts, the dealership ditched the collection boxes and instead challenged themselves to fill a car in the showroom to the brim with donations.

With the help of employees and customers, not only did they exceed their goal by stuffing 201 toys into the festive Acura, but they even had an overflow of plushies to ride on top. Even Santa doesn’t pack his sleigh that tight!

The toys will be redistributed to children across the Philadelphia area this holiday season, putting smiles on faces and warming hearts all around. ❤️‍🔥
