Khan’s Con

Carscoops

Najeeb Khan, a 70-year-old former payroll entrepreneur, just earned himself a 97-month tour of the big house for a check-kiting scheme that netted him a cool $150M. 🪁

Khan's loot went into an elaborate car collection worth millions that would make even Jay Leno weep, featuring everything from vintage Ferraris to Jaguars.
