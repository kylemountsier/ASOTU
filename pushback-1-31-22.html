AUTOMOTIVE
Hiring From Outside Automotive Is A Hit
 
 
Kevin Grant, GM of Keys Hyundai of Mission Hills in LA decided to “Stop looking for car people” and instead “...start hiring for personality”. Working with their recruiter, they started setting up interviews targeting people in the hospitality, food/beverage, and skilled trades industries with much success. With success stories building and these ‘outside hires’ being promoted, the store is poised to continue serving customers with the enhanced customer experience retail shoppers have come to expect.
 
Telling it like it is - New strategy: Order Uber Eats every day…find our next hire!
AUTOMOTIVE
Tesla Focuses On Maximizing Output Amidst Record Earnings
 
 
At 2.4 Billion in profits, Tesla closed the year with a record quarter and their first back-to-back profitable year since they were founded in 2003. While their new Texas factory and its upcoming German counterpart are expected to double production, the car company has announced no new models and delayed their eagerly awaited Cybertruck another year. Citing a supply chain bottle-neck and having "enough on their plate" Tesla CEO, Elon Musk, has set a goal of increasing output before increasing options.
 
Telling it like it is - Some products need no update to sell: Ketchup. Bandaids. EVs? We'll see.
