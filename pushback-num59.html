NASA To Slam a Spacecraft Into An Asteroid
 
 

In September of next year, the plan is to minimally slow the speed of a small asteroid named Dimorphos, which will adjust its orbital period around a larger asteroid by just a few minutes. Though there are no implications for earth with this set of asteroids 6.8 million miles away, it will be a powerful study in our ability to affect objects in space.
