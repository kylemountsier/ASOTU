The confusing part. Despite the month and year of growth, Q3 is down 4.2% compared to 2021.

GM Slows Its Return to Office
 
 
In another painful reminder that we can't all be Elon Musk, GM has delayed the timeline for forcing workers back to the office.
 
A memo released by GM execs called the announcement's late Friday afternoon timing unintentionally unfortunate. The new plan is to leave the "work appropriately" model alone until Q1 2023. The memo also stated the company will not mandate what days employees must come to the office.
