Rising Interest Rates Drive Higher Auto Loans Payments
 
 
According to the US Bureau of Labor Statistics, new-car prices are up 12.5% year over year, with used car prices rocketing 35.3% in the same time frame.
 
Enter the Fed. The Federal Reserve is boosting key interest rates by .5%, including auto loans. Automakers have subsidized past interest rates, but experts expect the size of the hike to impact new and used monthly car payments.

AUTOMOTIVE
Nikola Has Impressive First Quarter
 
 
US Heavy-truck EV maker Nikola surpassed Wall Street projections for Q1. Its first deliveries went out in April of this year, and the company reports orders for 500 move BEV Tre trucks. The company expects to ship between 300 and 500 semi trucks this year.
 
Supply and Stock. The company has announced sufficient resources for the proposed vehicles, which led to a 7% jump in market value.

AUTOMOTIVE
Volkswagen Group Invests In Spain
 
 
VW will invest 10B euros (about $10.5B) in electrifying Spain. The plan marks the largest industrial investment ever made in Spain. The Spanish government shares VW's dream of making Spain a European EV hub.
 
Coming soon. According to Herbert Diess, CEO of VW Group, plans include:
  • A battery gigafactory.
  • Updates to existing plants.
  • Investing in a comprehensive supplier ecosystem. 
Telling it like it is — OEMs are making major moves towards these 2030 EV goals. We wonder how those who wait until 2029 to adapt will make out.
