#Marlow75ActsOfKindness
 
 
Marlow Automotive Group has been serving the communities of Front Royal, Luray, and Winchester, Virginia for 75 years!
 
To celebrate the history and honor the ongoing commitment to service, the Marlow team has taken an "all gas, no brakes" approach to giving back. Under Emily Marlow Beck's leadership, the group will perform 75 Acts of Kindness in their community. From supporting a local food drive, to a pet adoption event, and even getting down in the dirt to redo landscaping for a local non-profit, they have listened to the community and are partnering to make it a more beautiful, connected, and loving place.
 
You can follow the action by searching for #Marlow75ActsOfKindness on Linkedin. Marketing Director Brian Nieves is uploading pictures of smiling faces and opportunities to join the group in their efforts to serve their community well.

Lexus's Secret Mountain Head Office
 
 
Lexus is opening a new hidden headquarters in the forest-covered mountainous region outside Tokyo in early 2024. The company has not directly commented on why it sounds like the beginning of a spy movie, but we assume it is all above board.
 
The welcome mat says, "How did you find us?" The headquarters will act as a home for the company's push into the next era of EV development. A test track has been operational at the site since 2019. Due to tree coverage and quiet electric motors, even that can remain unnoticed.
