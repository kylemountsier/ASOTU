Sinking Aces With UK Dealers

Giphy

As we all know, things change when you cross the Atlantic. Unfortunately, some things like sickness stay the same, but luckily so do people willing to help.

Corkills Northwich Hyundai dealer in the UK hosted a charity golf event, unlike most of those we've covered. Instead of just walking around in the hot sun all day(or rain since this is the UK), folks who pitched in twenty to support The Joshua Tree were eligible for a hole-in-one-prize.

The Joshua Tree is a charity group that supports children facing cancer and their families. The 60 players brought in almost $5,000 for the critical work The Josuha Tree does.

One lucky player hit the hole-in-one and drove home in a brand-new Ioniq 6! Proving that while golf may be a good walk spoiled , sometimes it is a good drive made better.
