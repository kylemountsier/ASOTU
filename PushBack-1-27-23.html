Toyota CEO Steps Aside
 
 
Toyota's CEO is stepping down as the head of his grandfather's company and assuming the role of chairman. The current head of Lexus will take over as of April 1.
 
Investors say Toyoda was a good leader, but they are interested in seeing if the new CEO will take the company beyond past insistence on hybrids and hydrogen vehicles.

NYC May Mandate Electric Ride Shares
 
 
New York City proposed a rule to require all Uber and Lyft operations in the city to go electric by 2030.
 
As one of the largest markets for ride-share platforms, the rule could impact an estimated 100K vehicles.
 
The companies that have sued over regulations say they are excited to partner with NYC in this transition.
 
Telling it like it is - If an Uber driver uses an autonomous vehicle, who keeps the tip?

BMW’s Solid-State Battery Plan
 
 
BMW is redirecting its attention back to solid-state battery technology. Through a partnership with Solid Power, the automaker (and Ford in a separate deal with Solid Power) will try to bring the previously unrealized battery tech to market.
 
Training and Tests. The partnership will not produce anything for consumers yet. BMW engineers will learn from Solid Power, and later this year the battery company will send solid-state batteries to BMW for testing. BMW's 2025 Nueu Klasse EVs will not use solid-state batteries, but instead feature upgraded lithium-ion that offers a 20% energy density improvement.
 
Telling it like it is - The EV competition is one part poker game and one part jigsaw puzzle. BMW is entering a jigsaw phase, but not folding their hand.
