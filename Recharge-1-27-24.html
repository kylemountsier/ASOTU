SOMETHING FUN

Tesla Bot Goes Full Rambo on a Cybertruck

X / Supercar Blondie

Podcaster Alex Lorusso recently dropped a viral video that’s got the whole internet doing a double-take. 👀 👀

The 33-second video shows Tesla robots packing some serious heat and taking shots at a Cybertruck.

The scene is as wild as it sounds. One robot hands another robot donning a cowboy hat a Tommy Gun, who then proceeds to unload a hailstorm of bullets into the driver-side of the heavy-duty electric truck.

The video culminates in a moment of tongue-in-cheek humor as the robot driver of the Cybertruck emerges from the vehicle unscathed, proclaiming “I’m alive!”

For now, this scene was completely created in a digital space using CGI, but it’s probably only a matter of time.

Though his enthusiasm was underwhelming, the realistic video even drew the attention of Elon Musk who did leave a complimentary comment on the video with one succinct word: “Nice.”
