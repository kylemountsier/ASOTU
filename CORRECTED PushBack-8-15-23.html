Dahl Brings Dinner

Giphy/Boomerang Official

Dahl Automotive came together with its community to make a difference in the lives of the hungry.

The Month of Giving: During July, Dahl Auto donated a portion of sales from used cars and oil changes, raising an impressive $10,000.

Food for the Hungry: The money went to food for the Hunger Task Force, the area's only free food bank, supporting 120 different food programs.

A Community Effort: Shelly Fortner, the food bank's director, praised group President Andrew Dahl and the Dahl automotive team for the impact of its support.

Shout out to the Dahl team! Nobody wants their neighbor to go hungry, but folks often stop at wishing. It takes special care to do something about it.
