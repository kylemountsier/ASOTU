DEALERS

The Power of Perseverance and Originality

Matthews

After three years of hard work and anticipation, Matthews Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram in Great Bend, Pennsylvania, is finally ready for its debut.

Despite facing numerous pandemic challenges and inventory shortages, the dealership has persevered. Their 25K square-foot facility underwent extensive renovations, bringing over 33 new jobs to the rural town.

The Matthews family’s first dealership was a Chrysler Plymouth store that opened in 1973. They have a special attachment to this new location and are excited about its prospects.

Especially considering one of the facility’s most unique features— an obstacle course for Jeeps , which allows customers to test the vehicles' off-road capabilities before purchasing. It’s only one of two in the whole state.

Telling it like it is - We’re not gonna lie, we’re pretty jealous of all the Pennsylvanians who get to test-drive on that obstacle course. ASOTU team field trip??

TECHNOLOGY

Laser Downloads, Enzyme Discos, and Artistic Discretion

Giphy/Warner Bros.

Fiber < Laser. No more buffering blues, folks! The big brains at NASA and MIT have blown previous records out of the water, achieving warp-speed internet, thanks to their TeraByte InfraRed Delivery (TBIRD) system! Imagine downloading 1,000 high-def movies in less time than it takes to brew a pot of coffee. At over 200 Gbps , the laser link sets a new record and helps us imagine a future with lasers we can trust. Eventually, YouTube videos may load as fast as YouTube ads.

On the one hand, "Space Laser Internet" is the sort of thing that a James Bond villain dreams about, but on the other, this internet may be fast enough to finally let us send holograms to the office in our place.

Giphy/Mascista

Disco is dead. A plastic-eating enzyme could be the answer to tons and tons of landfill and ocean-dwelling waste. The first item on the menu is polyester clothing! Scientists say even heavily dyed fabrics could be digested without the energy-wasting methods of some current recycling efforts. Just don't get any on your morning jog.

On the one hand, we need to do something about all this plastic. Still, on the other, so many things are made from plastic and untamed enzymes may undo our society.

Giphy/Diggs

Generation VS Creation? A sci-fi author is living up to his genre with a wild 97 published mini-novels in 9 months. He says the "AI Lore Books" series is a testament to the potential of AI for augmenting human creativity. He mostly combined chatbots like ChatGPT with the image generator Midjourney to create his illustrated 5000-ish-word novels. So far, he has made $2,000 and sold over 500 copies of the interconnected stories.

On the one hand, art is discretion: releasing only what appeals to the artist in a way they believe will translate. On the other hand, if 20 people do this, nobody will be able to find E-books worth reading on Amazon.
