Jeep Announces Drive to Electrified Freedom
 
 

In a recent press release, Jeep announced the "100% Electrified Freedom" event. They will allow international media members to drive their Jeep Compass and Renegade with new e-Hybrid tech. 

A new mountain to climb. The event supports the company's push toward zero emissions while not sacrificing the style and performance Jeep fans enjoy. The company plans to launch their first fully electric SUV at the beginning of 2023 with most of its traditional ICE business in Europe at the end of this year.
 
Telling it like it is - $5 on Jeep being the first EV company to let drivers recharge with lightning bolts.
AUTOMOTIVE
Shift to Acquire Fair's dealer listing marketplace.
 
 
Tuesday, online used-vehicle retailer Shift Technologies said it would buy the dealer listing marketplace assets of Fair Technologies for $15M and 2.5% equity. Shift said it wants to establish an online marketplace presence where dealers and independent sellers can list their cars alongside Shift's inventory. They want the marketplace launched by Q2. 

Shortcut! Shift CEO George Arison said they estimated it would have taken two years to build a marketplace, so buying Fair's existing technology was the quicker move for their long-term goals.
 
Telling it like it is - Is Shift's marketing going to work for dealers, or is the dealer's inventory going to work for Shift? 🤔

Bitcoin Hardware Maker Announced Stock Buybacks
 
 
Canaa is one of the largest bitcoin mining hardware makers. This week they announced a program to buy back up to $100M in new stocks due to the impact of international frictions, Covid-19, and "macro factors across the capital market."

All part of the plan. After the announcement Tuesday morning, Canaan stocks shot up 30% on the NASDAQ. CEO Nangeng Zhang said the program was an effort to use the company's strong cash position to drive value for the shareholders.

Lyft Adding a Fuel Surcharge
 
 
The rideshare app, Lyft, will add a $0.55 surcharge to each ride next week. The surcharge will last for at least 60 days as gas prices continue to be an issue. 

Somebody has to pay for it. While the surcharge attempts to help drivers, the bulk of the cost is the customers'. Another company, DoorDash, announced a cash-back program to assist drivers with fuel issues without increasing the cost to customers.

MARKET
S&P 500 Hits a "Death Cross"
 
 
2022 has been a poor year for the S&P 500, and the index has reached a scarily low level. Currently, stock prices have fallen below their long-term trend regarding risks to the economy.  

On the bright side. Amid the challenges, investors see opportunity. The "Death Cross" is when the index's 50-day moving average falls below the 200-day number. Stocks can remain in this low place for some time, but as investors buy into the long-term value of an eventual rebound, the value recovers.
 
Telling it like it is - Stock market 101 is buying low. Stock market 102 is not spilling coffee on your sweet ties.
