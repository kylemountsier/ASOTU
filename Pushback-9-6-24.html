giphy

As the traditional “college-for-all” model faces increasing skepticism, more young adults are choosing to enter the workforce directly rather than pursue a degree. This shift presents a unique opportunity for auto dealers to tap into a growing talent pool.

Already grappling with staffing shortages, the auto industry can capitalize on this trend by offering vocational training, apprenticeships, and career development paths. As demand for cars—especially used cars—rises, dealerships can attract young workers seeking fulfilling careers without the burden of student debt.
