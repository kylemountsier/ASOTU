Parts And Service Recover
 
 
Most categories of dealership service and parts business have recovered from Covid-19 shutdowns. Miles traveled on US roads was up 10.6% in Feb 2022 year-over-year.
 
Lithia Motors reports customer pay service work increased 15% and body-shop work by 5% in Q1 vs. 2021. Warranty work, however, is down 3% due to new vehicle volume remaining low.

Saudi Government Orders 100,000 Vehicles From Lucid
 
 
Saudi Arabia is a significant investor in EV start-up Lucid. The company received a boost from the Saudi government, which agreed to purchase up to 100K vehicles over the next ten years.
 
Up to? The deal will guarantee 50K purchases, with an option for an additional 50K left over to the Ministry of Finance. Deliveries will begin next year.

CRYPTO
Fort Worth Becomes First US City to Mine Bitcoin
 
 
Fort Worth city council leaders unanimously approved a bitcoin mining operation using machines donated by the Texas Blockchain Council. The program will use three S9 mining machines 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The city estimates energy consumption compared to a household vacuum for each device, generating about $0.78/ea a day in cryptocurrency.
 
Moving forward. Lawmakers will evaluate the program in 6 months. Proponents of the plan say they have been considering the future of their economy since the financial crisis in 2008.
 
Telling it like it is —  Crypto mining requires cheap energy and space. Texas has both.

MARKETING
Google Earnings Dip, Signaling Online-Ad Woes
 
 
Google owner Alphabet Inc. reported Q1 earnings under Wall Street estimates. CFO for the parent company, Ruth Porat, says Q2 could be even slower.
 
Blame Russia... The company blames the earning shortcomings on the Russian invasion of Ukraine, but analysts are not so sure. With new competitors, such as TikTok, impacting the online advertising industry, experts are hesitant to call the change temporary.

Super Mario Bros. Movie Delayed
 
 
Nintendo's new Super Mario movie will still star Chris Pratt but will release four months later than previously announced.
 
Why? Nintendo director, Shigeru Miyamoto, did not say why the movie was delayed. Speculation points to the new release data lining up with the opening of the US Super Nintendo World theme park.
