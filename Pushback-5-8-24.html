Wholesale Data Wanes

Despite wholesale used-vehicle prices  declining in April , the automotive industry has shown resilience across most market segments. 

  • Manheim Used Vehicle Value Index (MUVVI) dropped to 198.4, a 14.0% year-over-year decrease.

  • April's daily sales conversion rate was 59.6%.

  • Retail used-vehicle sales in April decreased by 4% from March.

  • Rental risk unit prices fell 12.2% year over year.

  • Consumer Confidence Index declined by 5.9% in April.

Wholesale: because you can't sell half a car. 

 

Lucid's Q1 Leap

Lucid Motors  has reported a significant increase in Q1 2024  deliveries and revenue, demonstrating steady progress as it gears up to launch new electric vehicle models later this year.

  • Lucid Motors Q1 2024: Delivered 1,967 Air sedans, a 13% increase from Q4 2023.

  • Q1 revenue reached $172.7 million, up from $149.4 million in Q1 2023.

  • Produced 1,727 BEVs in Q1, down 27% from Q4 2023.

  • Net losses decreased to $680.9 million from $779.5 million year-over-year.

  • Ended Q1 with $5.03 billion liquidity.

 

Ferrari's Hydrogen Future

Ferrari has  patented a hydrogen-powered  internal combustion engine, exploring an innovative approach to sustain combustion engines under stringent future emission regulations.

  • Ferrari files patent for hydrogen-powered internal combustion engine.

  • Hydrogen engines emit no CO2 and minimal other pollutants.

  • Patent highlights challenges: less energy-dense than gasoline, requires more storage.

  • Concerns about hydrogen production's carbon footprint.

  • Hydrogen infrastructure limitations are similar to those faced by fuel-cell vehicles.

 

Nissan's Collaborative Newness 

Nissan  partners with ACCIONA  to launch the Silence S04 electric cars and motorcycles in Europe, enhancing sustainable urban mobility with impressive vehicle ranges.

  • Nissan and ACCIONA partnership introduces Silence S04 electric vehicles.

  • The product range includes quadricycles and motorcycles.

  • Sales start in France and Italy in June 2024 and Germany in September.

  • Vehicles offer 149 km to 175 km range, with speeds up to 85 km/h.

  • Features include battery swapping and removable packs for charging.

Motorbikes: because sometimes you CAN sell half a car...

MORE THAN CARS MOMENTS

Making Mobile Count

Bill Knight Automotive/LinkedIn

President of Bill Knight Automotive, Bill Knight, shared a wonderful story on  LinkedIn  recently. 

He and his team do something called "Mobile Tuesday," and in a slight break from the norm, they ventured out to the Tulsa Domestic Violence Intervention Shelter to change oil and wiper blades free of charge for the residents and employees.

Safe, reliable, and affordable transportation is a massive barrier to overcoming the injustice in the lives of abuse survivors and their children. 

Mr. Knight finished his post by saying he “couldn't be prouder" of his team, and we agree. We are proud every day to serve an industry with people like Bill and his team in it.
