October with Beaver Toyota
 
 
Beaver Toyota of Cumming collected a portion of every vehicle sold in October to Bulldogs Battling Breast Cancer!
 
Friday, they donated a check for $52,000!
 
The Bulldogs Battling Breast Cancer will use the funds to continue their fight through early detection and assistance for women in need.
 
An Instagram post by Beaver Toyota sharing the story said, "We are blessed to have people like them (Bulldogs) in this world making strides to help fight this terrible disease."
 
We at ASOTU feel blessed knowing many in the dealer community, like the folks at Beaver Toyota of Cumming, who consider it a blessing to give in support of groups like this.
 
Telling it like it is - About 15 years ago, I had a flat tire in a borrowed pickup truck. A stranger stopped and not only used their tools to take the flat off but drove me and the spare to a tired shop to put air in it before putting it on the truck with their own hands. I think about them often, and every day I am a little more convinced it was a Beaver Toyota employee sent back in time, like in the Terminator movies.

Ford and GM Take Different Roads
 
 
Surprise! Automakers are trying to figure the world out in real time - just like the rest of us.
 
The challenges, goals, and present circumstances of each OEM are unique and, when added up, may give us a more complete story of the industry now and down the road.
 
üöôüñ• Ford recently backed off its autonomous vehicle project in favor of EVs and more imminent technologies. The brand will maintain ICE investment, but expects 40% of its sales to be electric by 2030.
