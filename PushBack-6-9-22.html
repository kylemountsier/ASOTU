Wholesale Used Prices Continue To Climb
 
 
A new report by Cox Automotive shows a 0.7% increase in used car prices from April to May and 9.7% from May the previous year. The new data brings the year-over-year increase to 12.1%, with all major market segments except pickups impacted by the rise. Total sales continue to be down due to ongoing supply issues.
 
Telling it like it is —  Did 0.7% of consumers also enter the “wait it out” camp from April to May?

Road Rules
 
 
If you have this email, you know the automotive industry touches basically every aspect of our nation. If you do not know that: Howdy, welcome to ASOTU!
 
Staying aware of where OEMs, Dealers, Vendors, and Lawmakers agree with or challenge each other can often be a preview of things to come.
 
✅ 🏭Five automakers support the EPA's reinstatement of California's authority regarding emission standards. Seventeen attorneys general sued the EPA to have the waiver revoked. Ford, BMW, Honda, Volkswagen, and Volvo filed a motion to intervene in the federal lawsuit.
 
🌎 👀The US asked Mexico to investigate worker rights violations at a Stellantis parts plant. The complaint stems from workers saying the company has blocked their right to self-chosen representation in collective bargaining. This is the fourth such complaint under the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA).
 
⛽ 💤Some Honda Pilot SUVs are under investigation in the US. Customers complain the fuel-saving stop-start system stops... but then forgets to do the start part of the process. Nearly 195K vehicles from 2016 to 2020 are covered by the probe. 221 complaints have been filed, with no crashes or injuries reported.

WORLD NEWS
EU Steps Towards Banning ICE Sales
 
 
The EU's European Parliament voted in favor of a full ban on ICE cars sales by 2035. The decision still has to be examined, debated, and voted on by the European Council, which will take some time. A faction of the parliament voiced opposition to a 100% ban in favor of a 90% ban.
 
Full approval will likely result in a rapid transition to fully electric vehicles for EU consumers. Some lawmakers consider middle grounds such as synthetic fuels, while others imagine the implications for export markets such as Africa and Asia.
 
Quick Fact —  A change this big would require more than 5 automotive plants in Europe to be completely retooled or constructed.

Sonic The Hedgehog Can’t Catch A Break
 
 
Not too long ago Sonic the Hedgehog had a movie delayed by a few months because fans of the series hated the CGI hero’s look so much the production company completely redid it. Now upcoming video game Sonic: Frontiers is facing a similar sort of shade as fans say its preview footage is visually and mechanically boring.
 
Maybe it is early footage? The game is six months from launch and the hands-on demo footage, which you’d expect to be the most wowing aspects of the game, are mostly just “meh.”
 
Telling it like it is —  It's like they took all the dragons, towns, and swords out of Skyrim and dropped Sonic in there and called it a game.
