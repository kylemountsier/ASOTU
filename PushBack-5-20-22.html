GM Moves Toward Full EV Portfolio
 
 
GM President Mark Reuss says the company has learned a lot since its early EV1 was released. Reuss says the company was afraid when they released the Chevy Bolt in 2016 and "didn't get behind it."
 
Learning from the past. GM has its eye on a $35B investment in EV and autonomous vehicles through 2025, with plans for an all-electric portfolio by 2035. Reuss says the company is ready to "really go at it."
 
Telling it like it is —  Whoa, did GM just say “y’all won’t catch us slippin again,” in the most business-like way possible?

VW Set To Win Big Rental Car Deal
 
 
A consortium led by Volkswagen Group is nearing approval for a $3B bid for French car rental company Europcar. The deal will increase VW's presence in the lucrative mobility service sector. The EU has to approve the takeover per antitrust laws, but people close to the matter say the deal will go through.
 
Europcar. The car rental company has a presence in 140 countries and a fleet of over 350,000 vehicles. VW will earn revenue from rentals, but also want to acquire more car and consumer data.
 
Telling it like it is —  Europcar is present in 140 of the 195 countries in the world. If collecting data is the goal, VW won this deal.

Tesla Dropped From S&P 500 ESG List
 
 
Investors use the ESG list to choose companies based on shared environmental, social, and governance values. The newest list has removed Tesla based on claims of lacking a low carbon strategy, amassed complaints regarding working conditions and racial discrimination, and mishandled investigations into injury and death involving the car's autopilot feature.
 
To Twitter. Musk called out the list and Tesla's exclusion as a scam, saying phony social justice warriors have weaponized it. He pointed out other companies on the list, including JPMorgan, the largest lender to the fossil-fuel industry, and Amazon, which is embroiled in labor disputes.
 
Telling it like it is —  We'd like to make a joke here about Elon employing the "but everybody's doing it" defense, but that list is legit weird.
