US Consumers Are Surpassing Financial Obligations
 
 
Payment amounts are a trustworthy indicator of financial struggle. TransUnion data show US consumers are maintaining monthly payments over the required minimums across industries, including automotive. TransUnion says over-the-minimum payments on leases, loans, and credit cards are a good sign that the average household is above water.
 
Telling it like it is —  Paying off a loan early is like mailing your future self money.

Illinois Issues Temporary Block On Carvana
 
 
Due to ongoing complaints against online auto retailer Carvana, the Secretary of State Police in Illinois has temporarily blocked its dealer license. The complaints stem from improperly transferred titles and the misuse of out-of-state temp permits.
 
Last year the company received a 180-day suspension in North Carolina for similar practices, but Illinois has not established a timeline for the block.

AUTOMOTIVE
Toyota Experiments With Heavy-Duty Hydrogen Tech
 
 
Toyota launched a hydrogen-powered sedan in 2014 and has continued developing the tech since. The company announced it would seek close European cooperation to apply the technology to buses and heavy-duty trucks.
 
Opposition. Others in the automotive/energy space, such as Elon Musk, have voiced doubts about hydrogen fuel cells due to production costs and the current need for green hydrogen in other industries.
 
Telling it like it is —  After the last few months we aren’t sure any one power source is the whole picture for the future. Wouldn’t be surprised if they just started adding pedals at some point.

TECH
Hackers Develop Bluetooth Hijacking
 
 
Some custom code and $100 in hardware are all hackers need to access your Bluetooth signals. The hijacking method is a threat to many conveniences Bluetooth offers, but most noteworthy is the ability to access Tesla's phone-based unlocking option.
 
Ok, but what is the solution? Currently, Tesla has no public statements or announced plans for patches or solutions. Lock-maker Keikset has announced plans for app timeouts with PIN requirements to enable unlocking access. That solution is available on iOS and should be applied to Android by September.
 
Telling it like it is —  We’re just going to add Bluetooth to the list of things we mostly leave off. Right next to ringtones.
CULTURE
US Soccer Agrees To Standardized Pay
 
 
The US Soccer Federation will pay its men's and women's teams equally in a landmark agreement. The decision comes after negotiations between the federation and the two unions.
 
Moving forward, both unions will participate in revenue sharing for commercial, merchandising, and World Cup player bonuses.
