Giving Week with DeNooyer Automotive Family
 
 
The DeNooyer Automotive Family is having its annual giving week! This year they will partner with three local community groups to give their time, resources, and support however they can.
 
In addition to giving, each store in the family will also receive. Members of the community groups will be making rounds to each location to share knowledge and their passion for the work with the whole store in speaking events, further inviting each individual into a community-centered collaboration.
 
The groups are BOYS & GIRLS CLUB OF GREATER KALAMAZOO. INC, Kalamazoo Loaves & Fishes, and Kalamazoo Gospel Ministries.
 
Telling it like it is — We love that these partnerships and these weeks dedicated to the community are not one-off situations in the dealer ecosystem but a recurring part of the rhythm. That is who dealers are and we are here for it. Great work Erin Sparks and the whole DeNooyer family!

GM and Hertz Want To Rent You An EV
 
 
🤝General Motors and Hertz are teaming up to expand the number of electric vehicles available to rent.
 
🤑GM will sell 175K EVs to Herts over the next five years. Currently, Hertz's fleet is about 500K total vehicles.
 
🚗The EVs will come from all of GM's brands, including their commercial vehicle division, BrightDrop.
 
💪This is the largest deal of its kind.
 
Telling it like a snarky cowboy—  That’s an awful lotta cars for somebody “dealing with a global supply shortage.” 🤠

Ford Issues A Warning
 
 
Ford says inflation-related costs would be a crisp $1B more than expected this quarter. The market responded by dropping Ford shares 11%, putting the automaker on track for its worst day in 11 years.
 
An 11% drop in Ford's value would account for a $7B loss from the company's market value.
 
Telling it like it is — We keep looking at this, and each way we turn it it seems the market is punishing Ford for telling the truth. Weird.

China, The Moon, and Nuclear Power
 
 
🥼Helium-3 is an isotope with significant potential implications for nuclear fusion power's future.
 
🚀A Chinese mission to the moon just returned successfully with some freshly harvested Changesite-(Y), a colorless mineral packed with Helium-3.
 
💡As opposed to other methods of producing fusion power, Helium-3 produces no radioactive neurons.
 
🧑‍🚀Several more missions are planned, resulting in a manned mission to the lunar surface in 2030.
 
Telling it like it is — One small step toward energy abundance on Earth, one giant leap toward affordable apartments on the Moon.
