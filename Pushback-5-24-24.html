Welcome Back To Saturday!

In the last few weeks, we’ve been so focused on ASOTU CON that we took a break from our usual Saturday recaps. Let us reacquaint ourselves with the present as it reveals a future where looking at the past is normal again.

The Daily Pushback Email

  • On Monday , we looked at a “flying car” (PSST, it’s an EV with a Drone on its back), shared some wholesale price data, and took a peek at Honda’s hydrogen semi-truck. We ended the email with a bonkers little story about 700+ people named Kyle trying to set a world record.

  • Then, on Tuesday , we saw some import issues: the UAW was voted down in Alabama, and VW rolled back some efforts. Check the bottom for a photo dump from ASOTU CON and a bit of windshield-sized tech news.

  • By Wednesday , we turned our attention toward Polestar’s NASDAQ drama, Fisker dropped roadside assistance, and Ford told dealers to take a beat. After that, we shared a story from Hendrick Automotive Group. The group and its 11K associates grabbed a Glassdoor award for being a great place to work.

  • Thursday , we celebrated crossing 10K followers on LinkedIn before sharing about California policies and Chinese tariffs. We shared a short write-up of Will Guidara’s closing keynote from ASOTU CON 2024. It was pretty dope.

  • Finally, on Friday, we had some good news for dealers, slow news from Nissan, and weird news from Pairs (they wanna Facetime drive your car). We ended the day and week with a bit of data showing the average age of personal vehicles has continued to increase.

Fancy Dogs

Are you, like me, only vaguely aware of what the Met Gala is? It's ok. The Met Gala is a fashion show fundraiser where celebrities dress like comic book convention rejects to raise money for the Costume Institute at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City. 

ANYWAY, we want to talk about the Pet Gala , where two dozen dogs dressed up like Gala-clad celebrities and went for a walk. (woof) 

Why? Just to make people happy. The designer, Anthony Rubio, said he wanted to bring happiness to people amid global negativity.
