AUTOMOTIVE
Investment Firm To Acquire CDK Global in Q3
 
 
CDK Global has agreed to be acquired by Brookfield Business Partners for $8.3B. The deal will pay shareholders $54.87 per share and take the DMS giant private.
 
Both companies released enthusiastic statements regarding the deal and the future of retail automotive.
 
Y’all hear that? The sound of a lot of red tape being peeled off?
AUTOMOTIVE
Toyota Boosts Autonomous Driving Development
 
 
Toyota's autonomous driving unit, Woven Planet,  is deploying its cameras to outside companies. The company hopes the collected data will advance autonomous driving technology faster than they can alone. Until now
 
Cameras Vs. Sensors. Tesla has been experimenting with Woven Planet's inexpensive cameras with success. Previous efforts to develop fully autonomous technology have relied on sensors, but camera-based technology may eventually overtake advanced sensors. Elon Musk claimed Tesla would achieve full autonomy using the new tech within a year.
 
Telling it like it is — We’re crossing our fingers and adding a tally mark next to “Tesla has self-driving figured out” on our wall.
AUTOMOTIVE
Jeep Pulls Ahead in Auto Ad Ranking
 
 
March means basketball, and basketball means ad spending. Jeep spent an estimated $13.7M on ads during the March Madness tournament. According to iSpot data, it was money well spent, as the company's ads scored an average 13.2% higher than industry norms.
 
Top five. Other high scoring ads came from Ram, Hyundai, Cadillac, and Kia.
 
Telling it like it is — Jeep should just start paying Jeep owners for the advertising they’re constantly doing...
CRYPTO
NFT Game Developer Raises Funds For Hacked Users
 
 
Last month the Ronin Validator Hack purged over $625M in crypto from the Axie Infinity ecosystem. Axie Infinity is a game where players buy and use NFTs as a key element in gameplay. The hack affected crypto and NFTs owned by users and the developer, Sky Mavis.
 
Ouch. After ensuring security measures, Sky Mavis raised $150M to reimburse users and will make up the rest from their funds. The company continues working with law enforcement to recover the stolen funds and conduct an independent investigation.

WHAT THE KIDS ARE INTO
r/Place?
 
 
This year Reddit brought back the worldwide group art project, r/Place. Site users joined a collaborative digital painting session that lasted multiple days. The massive image morphed through memes, flags, and chaotic explosions of colors as individuals were given a single pixel to place every ten minutes.
 
When is the next one? No timeline for another event has been announced, which adds to the mystery and fun of r/Place for many participants. The first r/Place was back in 2017 and wrapped up in a single day. This year's event started on April 1 and stretched over several days after being announced only days before.
