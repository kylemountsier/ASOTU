GM's Shift Away from Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

  • Safety Focus : GM  phases out  Apple CarPlay and Android Auto in new EVs for safety.

  • Integrated System : Replaces with "Ultifi" software, featuring integrated Google apps.

  • Revenue Strategy : Move aligns with data management and in-car spending potential.

  • Commitment to Partnerships : Maintains ties with Apple and Google despite changes.

Historic Agreement at COP28 for Fossil Fuel Phase-Out

  • Global Commitment : Nearly 200 countries  agree  to transition away from fossil fuels.

  • Ambitious Targets : Aim for net-zero emissions by 2050 and tripling renewable energy by 2030.

  • First COP Recognition : Official acknowledgment of fossil fuels contributing to global warming.

  • Non-Binding Nature : Agreement criticized for loopholes; action and finance mobilization needed.

Toyota Partners with Cirba Solutions for Battery Recycling

  • Strategic Collaboration : Partnership  focuses  on recycling end-of-life EV batteries.

  • Cost and Emission Reductions : Up to 70% savings anticipated.

  • High Recovery Efficiency : Cirba Solutions to recover up to 95% of critical minerals.

  • Carbon Neutrality Goal : Aligns with Toyota's ambition for 2035.

Swedish Union's Stand Against Tesla Over Labor Rights

  • Labor Action : Union  halts  waste collection at Tesla's Swedish workshops.

  • Widespread Support : Movement gains backing from various sectors and international entities.

  • Collective Bargaining Demand : Focus on employee rights for wages and conditions.

  • Legal Challenge : Tesla faces a court case over license plate collection in Sweden.

Range Rover EV: Anticipating a 2024 Debut

  • Early Launch : JLR  advances  Range Rover EV launch to 2024.

  • Rigorous Testing : Undergoing extensive engineering tests and patent filings.

  • High Performance : Comparable to V8 Range Rovers with rapid charging feature.

  • Electrification Commitment : Part of JLR's goal to be electric-first by 2030.

MORE THAN CARS MOMENTS

Safe and Warm

Instagram/Justice for Orphans/Denooyer

💙 In a heartening display of community support and generosity,  DeNooyer Chevrolet has teamed up with Justice For Orphans  and the CarePortal Network to donate over 100 beds to children in the Capital Region this holiday season. This initiative highlights the power of collaboration and the impact businesses can have in addressing local social issues. 🛌

🎄 Joe DeNooyer, President of  DeNooyer Chevrolet , spoke about the initiative and how the public can get involved. "We're committed to making a difference in our community, especially during the holiday season," said DeNooyer. "We invite everyone to join us in this cause. By simply donating a twin bed set, preferably in neutral colors, you can help make a child's holiday season a bit brighter." 😴

The dealership is inviting its community to participate in the giving by accepting donations such as twin sheets, mattress protectors, and bed-in-a-bag sets. 

Shout out to DeNooyer Chevrolet for your deep commitment to your community. It sets an inspiring example for other businesses. Through such initiatives, they strengthen community ties and address crucial needs, making the holiday season more comfortable and joyous for children who might otherwise go without. 

 
TIE CUTTERS

2024 TIME Dealer of the Year Nominee:
Steve Hinchcliff

Steve Hinchcliff, a third-generation car dealer from Nebraska, is a distinguished nominee for the TIME Dealer of the Year award at the 107th annual National Automobile Dealers Association Show in Las Vegas. Chosen by the Nebraska New Car & Truck Dealers Association, he stands out among 49 nominees for this prestigious accolade, highlighting the top auto dealers in the U.S. with a deep commitment to community service. Leading the H+H Group, which includes multiple dealerships and the H+H Family Foundation, Hinchcliff is celebrated for creating a positive workplace and actively contributing to community development, particularly in youth mentorship and career training.

Congratulations from the ASOTU team Steve! Your dedication to the auto industry and your profound impact on the community truly set a standard for excellence. We wish you continued success and commend your inspiring journey in the automotive world!

Festive Foodie Favors

Domino’s

Domino’s has vowed not to allow any pesky snowstorms to deter them from safety delivering you a fresh, hot pie this winter.

As part of their “ Plowing for Pizza” initiative, they're offering a cool half-million in community snow-plowing grants with twenty lucky cities receiving $25K each towards clear, safe roads.

Who knew a pizza chain would be the hero of winter road maintenance?
 

Pizza Hut

Domino’s isn’t the only pizza chain dishing out goodwill this season. Pizza Hut recently rolled out their creative “ reverse delivery” concept offering customers a free doormat with a scannable QR code.

The code allows delivery drivers from any company to receive a $25 Pizza Hut gift card as a token of appreciation during the bustling holiday season.
 

Starbucks

Adding a bit of festive cheer to your coffee experience, Starbucks is offering a sweet deal to its customers with a free (ish) short Signature Hot Chocolate on weekends through December.

We say “ish” because you have to buy a grande or larger to qualify for the free cocoa.

But, after all the oodles of money dropped on them a year, we’ll happily take any free thing they have to offer and consider it a win.
