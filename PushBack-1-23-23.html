DEALERS
Back To Basics Pt. 3
 
 
We all know that social media is one of the best ways to reach out to and stay engaged with your community. With so many platforms, algorithms and outlets, it's sometimes difficult to know where to start or how to keep up. This week's ATUC with David Long revealed how rapidly the social media landscape is changing and how to leverage those changes for more authentic content that better connects with your audience.
 
Get active. Engage first and reply often. Customers are less likely to start a conversation than to join one.
 
Get out of the box. In fact, throw the box away altogether. Boxes are so last year. This year is all about creative thinking and inventive solutions. More consumers are steering away from mass marketing and toward unique content.
 
Get prepared. Nothing will ever be more powerful than a human-to-human connection. With the advent of AI, content creators must adapt by cultivating their community, staying engaged, and providing unique content.
 
Get diverse. Gone are the days of Facebook and Google dominating the online market share. We must expand marketing initiatives to new frontiers like Reddit, YouTube Shorts, and dating apps. The sky's the limit!
 
Get efficient. Repurposing content is one of the easiest ways to work smarter, not harder. Tiny tweaks to your content help it feel fresh across platforms to reach more viewers.
 
Get direct. Ask for what you want, and be direct. Always include a call to action along with your content to draw viewers to your ecosystem.
 
Check out the full replay on Clubhouse and be sure to follow the room so you never miss a week!

General Motors and Tesla
 
 
 🏭🔌🧑‍🏭
GM announced $918M to be invested in plants for gas engines and EV components. Plants in Michigan, NY, and Ohio will receive upgrades to keep the brand's ICE portfolio strong, while pushing toward its all-electric future.
 
Wait, but didn't you say... The company announced a plan to phase out ICE vehicles by 2035, but some of the new investments will produce 6th-generation V8 gas engines.
 
Did you think one-way tickets to the all-electric future grew on trees?
🐢⏰📦
Tesla pushed back the delivery window of their Model 3 to Germany for a second time with a new timeframe of February-April 2023. The delay comes on the heels of news that Tesla's Berlin and Austin, TX plants were delayed by two months to meet their production forecast of 3,000 vehicles per plant per week.
 
Slower but still faster. Though distribution has been slower than expected, Tesla's delivery times are still far ahead of their competitors like Volkswagen, who can have wait times of up to 18 months.
 
TL: DR - GM’s investing in EVs and V8s and Tesla is hitting snooze on German deliveries (again).
CULTURE
Really? Chess?
 
 
Chess has seen a major resurgence in popularity over the past few years, but nothing has driven people to the chess boards quite like Mittens.
 
The super grandmaster chess-bot known as Mittens might look cute, but don’t let her wide eyes and cuddly appearance fool you. She is a sassy, stone-cold assassin in virtual fur, frequently taunting her opponents during matches with unsettling statements like, “Meow. I am become Mittens, the destroyer of kings. Hehehe.”
 
Gluttons for punishment. Since Mittens’ introduction on Chess.com at the beginning of January, it has seen a significant increase in its traffic, averaging 27.5 million games played daily (40% more than any other month in the company’s history).
