Motor Morsels: Audi, Detroit, Used EVs

Giphy

🤝 🤷‍♀️

Rumor has it. Audi is in talks with Chinese automaker SAIC to acquire its EV platform. The move to increase market share follows lagging Audi EV sales compared to competitors. Audi uses Volkswagen's MEB platform and is developing a new EV platform with Porsche, so it is unclear what impact this acquisition would have on the company's plans.

👎👍

Deal or no Deal? UAW President Shawn Fain launched aggressive negotiations with Detroit automakers over new worker contracts. Strikes could cost billions with potential weekly earning losses per automaker:

  • Stellantis — $470M

  • Ford — $620M

  • GM — $770M

Key issues include reinstatement of cost-of-living adjustments, increased wages, a platinum healthcare package retention, ending the tiered pay system, and setting guidance for electrification's impact on jobs .

📉🔋  

Ripples in the electric pond. Tesla's Model 3 saw a 30% drop in used car value over the past year, driving a significant fall in used EV prices in the US. This comes after price surges in 2022. Analysts attribute the dip to Tesla's new model price cuts, which have affected the entire used EV market. Tesla is 1st, 2nd, and 4th on ISeeCars ' list of EVs with the most significant value drops.

Colorful Cruisers

Porche

Make your rooms pop with a touch of Porche. The German carmaker is working with premium interior-paint brand Backdrop to color-match some of their iconic hues so you can bring their vibrant palette inside. The limited-edition partnership offers four heritage-inspired colors – Ruby Star, Irish Green, Riviera Blue, and Speed Yellow – each representing a significant chapter in Porsche's history.

At $75/gallon, we may stick with the color-matching department at Home Depot.  

 

FIAT

Rattan sedan. Okay, maybe it’s not technically a sedan, but it’s fun to say. In the spirit of the iconic FIAT 500, Bonacina and Garage Italia Customs have teamed up to create the FIAT 500 Spiaggina, a resto-mod masterpiece that harkens back to classic 50’s design. This electric-powered cutie features Bonacina signature rattan seats that are customized to match the paintwork.

Design your own 500 Spiaggina here ! 🎨

 

Mercedes-Benz

Back to the future. Mercedes-Benz's C-111 supercar was intended to rival Lamborghini and Ferrari models in the late 1960s and early '70s but never reached production…until now. Mercedes has introduced the Vision One-Eleven, an electric reinterpretation of the classic car with a futuristic twist and zesty orange paint job to match its “look at me!” vibes.

Best friends: Straight roads, lead footers, fans of pumpkins, carrots, or construction cones. Nemeses: Speed bumps, railroad tracks, XL gravel.
