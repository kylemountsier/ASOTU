USPS Increases EV Plans
 
 
The USPS will buy 66K EVs by 2028.
 
The announcement dramatically increases a previous goal of just 10% of the service's 50K - 165K fleet replacement vehicles being electric.
 
The vehicles will now be funded in part by the Inflation Reduction Act's allowances for updating 600K government vehicles to EVs.

Department of Treasury Pushes Back EV Tax Credit Updates
 
 
The treasury department will hold off on some aspects of the EV tax credit changes until March 2023.
 
Rules surrounding where battery materials are sourced will be held for later decisions. In contrast, other rules will begin on January 1.

Porsche Produces Clean Gas Alternative
 
 
Porsche is producing, not developing, producing, e-fuel that will keep ICE engines operating but at dramatically lower emission levels.
 
The fuel has been successfully tested in the brand's iconic Porsche 911. 

The company plans to produce millions of gallons of e-fuel by the middle of the decade.

GM: Fires and Recall
 
 
GM recalled 140K Chevrolet Bolts over carpet fire risks. In the case of a crash, models between 2017 - 2023 could experience a rare instance of the front seatbelt pretensioner exhaust gases coming in contact with the floor carpet fibers and causing a fire.
 
Most of the vehicles are in the US, with about 20K in Canada.

Batteries From The Sea
 
 
Sea Salt batteries could be the solution we didn't see coming!
 
With 4 times the capacity of lithium batteries and the needed material washing up on shore, the technology could power everything from phones to EVs.
 
Reducing mining and processing would combat the most significant impact EVs still have on the climate and environment.
