Beaver Toyota Charity Golf Event
 
 
Beaver Toyota in Cumming is the first dealership to achieve Daily PushBack email features in two consecutive weeks. (The trophy is in the mail, guys)

Uber Drives People Toward Electric
 
 
Uber's new EV option is live in 25 North American cities. The "Comfort Electric" option offers customers an upscale ride in a Tesla, Polestar, Mustang Mach-E, or similar higher-end EV.
 
✋First-hand experience with semi-luxury EVs may drive further adoption of the vehicles in population hubs like Atlanta, Austin, DC, Vegas, NY Suburbs, Philly, etc.
 
Ultimately Uber has its sights on zero emissions in the US and Canada by 2030. This also means the one million people who use the ride-share app as a source of income will be looking to invest in an electric vehicle within the next 7 or so years.
 
The question -  How ready are you to help an Uber driver (or ex Uber customer) switch to Comfort Electric?

Porsche’s IPO
 
 
Volkswagen is pushing on with plans to launch an IPO for its Porsche brand. VW is targeting 70-75B euros for the brand valuation, which is below investor estimates of up to 85B euros.
 
Announcing the target value and late Sept/early Oct timeline for the IPO's launch bumped VW's stake value 3%.
 
Telling it like it is — Porsche could be the #3 IPO in Europe after this. With OEMs looking to close the gap between now and their ambitious electric futures, will we see more of this brand launching?

Mercedes Captures The Wind
 
 
💨Mercedes-Benz is working on a wind farm in NW Germany.
 
⚡️By 2025, the company plans to cover 15% of its power needs in the country with wind.
 
🤨The plan includes an unspecified investment in the hundred millions with an unspecified energy partner.
 
☀️The company also works with local authorities to use an additional 800 hectares for solar power.
 
Telling it like it is —  In the long history of profit-protecting ideas, Self-owned renewable energy production seems like it would have happened sooner. Now that the technology is becoming more financially viable, it will be cool to see how it is adopted across the industry.

Rockstar Games Hacked
 
 
Rockstar Games confirmed reports of a sizable content leak were authentic. A hacker accessed pre-release gameplay footage of the studio's upcoming (and long-anticipated) GTA VI.
 
The developer says they are disappointed by the leak but will continue as planned with no real long-term damage expected.
