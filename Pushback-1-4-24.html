General Motors Leads US Sales in ‘23

GM maintained its status as the top-selling automaker in America in 2023, outpacing its closest competitor, Toyota, with impressive sales growth.

  • 📈 Sales Surge: GM's total U.S. vehicle sales rose 14.1%, leading an industry-wide upsurge to the highest sales since 2019.

  • 🌟  Standout Stars: Notably, Buick sales skyrocketed by 61%, and Chevrolet saw a 13.1% increase.

  • 🏗️ Overcoming Obstacles: Despite a costly auto strike, GM maintained its lead, contributing to nearly 2.6M vehicles sold  in the U.S. in 2023.

The Drone Zone

Giphy/Xbox

As we head deeper into the decade, we’re likely to hear a lot more buzzing in the skies as delivery drones are projected to become even more prevalent in the new year and beyond.

Data shows that as of 2022, drones are making over 2,000 commercial deliveries daily worldwide, and that number is consistently on the rise.

These delivery drones are making a real impact in hard-to-reach places and are way kinder to the environment than traditional vehicles. But, it's not all smooth sailing just yet.

The primary speed bump? Moolah.

Right now, labor costs are through the roof because of tight regulations, making up about 95% of the total delivery cost.

But, there's good news on the horizon. With tech getting smarter and cheaper, we're looking at a future where one person could manage a whole squad of drones. This could slash costs down to a couple of bucks per delivery, and major players like Amazon have already been gearing up to make it a reality.
