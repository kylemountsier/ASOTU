Weaving Compassion into the Holiday Season

Giphy/NBC

Zeigler Auto Group is back with its fifth annual Ugly Sweater Holiday Canned Food Drive, blending festive fashion with philanthropy.

Until December 22, 2023, all Zeigler dealerships across Michigan, Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin are collecting non-perishable food items, with each store choosing a local charity beneficiary.

Since its inception in 2019, the drive has consistently grown, reflecting Zeigler Auto Group's commitment to community support and making sure no one goes hungry during the holiday season.

Even amidst the 2020 pandemic, Zeigler's efforts were undeterred, collecting 13,500 items. Last year, the group amassed a remarkable 29,195 items, with Mercedes-Benz of Hoffman Estates leading for the third consecutive year.

CEO Aaron Zeigler celebrates this event as a cornerstone of community involvement and friendly competition among their extensive dealer group. The winning dealership gets the unique honor of donning ugly sweaters at a special party, a coveted prize for their commendable efforts.
