Carter Myers Automotive‚Äôs Healing September
 
 
Through September, Carter Myers Automotive collected money from each sale to support the University of Virginia Children's Hospital. After tallying up and printing a big ole check, our friend Liza Borches and her team delivered an astounding $25,500.00 to UVA!
 
The money will help with transportation needs, which Director of Development, Richard Long, says is one of the biggest challenges facing patients and their families.
 
Liza says the dealer group loves supporting the UVA doctors' and nurses' work for families in CMA's community.
 
We are lucky enough to have some background that helps it land in the special part of my heart.
 
Liza Borches talks about supporting every associate at Carter Myers Automotive as being key to the group's success. Which is where most business success stories roll the credits. But then you see a story like this and realize it doesn't stop.
 
The associate success that blossoms into store success is returned to the vulnerable among us because, at some level, associate success, store success, dealer group success, and community success are one wonderful progression of the dealer network's potential impact on the world.
 
Telling it like it is - And, just in time for Thanksgiving, CMA and UVA will host a Turkey Trot to raise even more money for the hospital! What a great way to build community partnership into the calendar!

BMW, Audi, and Stellantis
 
 
BMW plans to begin online direct-to-consumer sales soon. The Mini will be available online in 2024, with other BMW models following in 2026.
 
The company says they are in talks with its dealers but did not share the nature of the talks or hint at the dealer network's place in the online model.
 

Audi wants to put a $700 VR rig in your passenger seat. The Holoride system will work with 2023 Audi or newer to enjoy Netflix, YouTube, games, or one of the AR features in the system.
 
To avoid motion sickness, the system uses GPS and gyro data to move the virtual environment to match the rider's physical experience.
 
 
Stellantis says raw material inflation should ease next year. The inflated prices persisting from the Covid pandemic should lower as raw material catches and surpasses the backlog of demand.
 
The reduction in material costs should bring down prices across the industry. You know, if no new disruptions fall out of the sky.
 
TL; DR - BMW eyes the agency model, Audi puts video games in eye-wear, and Stellantis dries its eyes and says things are getting better.
