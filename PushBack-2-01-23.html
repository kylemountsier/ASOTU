Leader in Sustainability Award
 
 
Over NADA, Cox Automotive presented the 2023 Leader in Sustainability Award to Mark Miller Subaru of Utah. The award recognizes their exceptional efforts to reduce their carbon footprint, commit to renewable energy sources, and promote sustainability across their supply chain.
 
Their dedication doesn't stop there. Since 2010, the benefit corporation has donated over 3.2 million dollars to charities, valuing the community, and the environment over company profits.


"We are a fourth-generation business, and corporate responsibility has always been very important to us," Jeff Miller, GM and CEO, said as he accepted the award. "The idea of being an important steward of this community, doing the right thing always, taking care of our people and the planet; it's at the core of who we are and what we were founded on."

Telling it like it is - Having good ideals at our core is only useful if we act on them. Mark Miller Subaru’s core ideals are the useful kind!

Hertz Offers Try Before You Buy EVs
 
 
Hertz has teamed up with the city of Denver to launch an innovative EV and charging program designed to kill three birds with one stone:
  • Make EVs more accessible and promote their use.
  • Provide data to inform charging-station infrastructure for the city.
  • Develop Denver tech by providing EV tools and training through a local technical high school.

Try before you buy. The "Hertz Electrifies" program will let renters choose from a fleet of more than 5K EVs from various manufacturers with discounts and benefits. The partnership highlights the importance of collaboration between the private sector and local governments for EV adoption to evolve. They hope their program will serve as a model for similar projects around the country.
