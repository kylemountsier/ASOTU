EV Battery Swap Stations Are Back in Serious Consideration
 
 
Despite being declared a "Dead end" in 2015, battery quick swap stations have made a small but hopeful reappearance. Chinese auto company, NIO, installed 700 fully automated battery swap stations in 2021 and to date has replaced 5.3 million batteries for EV users. 

 

There are still challenges to face before battery swaps can replace, or even seriously supplement, the existing recharging infrastructure such as brand compatibility. But ultimately swapping may be the best way to solve for recharge times, battery replacement expenses, and new battery technology distribution as EVs become more advanced and common in the world.

Kevin Hart adds "Halloween" inspired 1969 Plymouth Roadrunner to his collection
 
 
Comedian, Actor, and avid classic car collector, Kevin Hart, has a new car. Hart commissioned automotive design company, Salvaggio Design, to turn the 1969 Roadrunner into a tribute to the muscle of the 1960s and horror movie, Halloween, naming the project "Michael Meyers".

At 940 horse-power the 7.0 liter supercharged hemi engine also features decked out pistons, crank, and heads, with an interior design to match. The black leather and orange stitching are accented with a knife-style shifter, a small image of the man himself, Michael Meyer, on the RPM gauge, as well as many other homages paid to the theme.

Credit Suisse upgrades Tesla to "outperform", stocks climb.
 
 
Swiss investment bank, Credit Suisse, announced Monday they have upgraded Tesla to "outperform" due to margin improvements and the promise of Full Self Driving features. The announcement drove a 10% increase in the company stock value. 

 

Tesla saw a 20% decrease in value over January after CEO, Elon Musk, announced there would be no new models from the company in 2022. Musk's plan to increase production of existing models and focus on other projects, such as FSD, caused some analysts to predict Tesla leading the EV market for the foreseeable future, even as other automotive companies increase their EV inventory.

Wordle rules everything around me
 
 
Since it was released in October 2021, the free word game, Wordle, has grown to a daily player base of 2.7 million players. Originally created by Josh Wardle for his partner, Wordle is a game that gives you 6 tries to guess a 5 letter word, but after each guess, the letters you used will be marked correct, in the wrong spot, or not in the word at all.
 
The game's steady rise to popularity is made more impressive by (and perhaps in part due to) its lack of ads, account creation, or personal data collection of any kind. Despite the nonexistent price tag, Wardle has said he feels a sense of responsibility to the players and especially enjoys having provided a way for folks to stay in touch despite the ongoing COVID pandemic.
