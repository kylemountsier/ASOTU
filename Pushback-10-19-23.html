DATA & INSIGHT

Fostering Fresh Thinking

Giphy

A dealership’s days are often filled with numbers and calculations and formulas (we’re sorry), but it’s important to remember the importance of creativity and experimentation. 🎨🧑‍🔬💭

Embracing innovation, fostering an atmosphere of inclusivity, and embracing the valuable lessons from successes and failures, are keys to staying ahead of the competition. Check out these ideas from a recent article on CBT News.

🔑  Benefits of a Creative Perspective:

  • Understanding new technologies, safety measures, and AI advancements is pivotal for staying ahead.

  • Innovative approaches facilitated by customer-centric feedback and data analysis can enhance the overall shopping experience.

🔑  Cultivating a Culture of Creativity:

  • Valuing employees' contributions fosters collaboration, boosts engagement, and leads to better decision-making.

  • Inclusive discussions, diverse viewpoints, and open brainstorming sessions generate innovative solutions.

🔑  Finding Comfort in Discomfort:

  • Creative problem-solving demands stepping out of the comfort zone and embracing uncomfortable challenges as opportunities for growth.

  • Dealerships should welcome new ideas, especially from their employees, and be open to innovative strategies and perspectives.

  • Fearlessly challenging the status quo, taking risks, and experimenting with new methods are key traits of successful problem solvers.

  • Pilot programs can serve as safe spaces for testing before widespread implementation.

🔑  Asking the Right Questions:

  • Dealerships need to understand why customers choose competitors and explore creative solutions to differentiate themselves in the market.

  • Deep insight into underlying problems and a broader contextual understanding are crucial for effective problem-solving.

  • Don’t know where to start? Turn to your team. They’re your greatest resource that often goes untapped when the right questions aren’t being asked.

🔑  Collaboration is Critical:

  • Working with suppliers to develop new products and partnering with other dealerships or companies for shared resources enhance innovation and expand your reach.

  • Engaging in meaningful dialogue with employees and vendors at all levels encourages diverse perspectives and imaginative strategies.

🔑  Overcoming Challenges:

  • Remember, making mistakes and learning from failures are integral parts of the creative problem-solving process.

  • Adapting strategies based on past experiences ensures informed decision-making and long-term success.

  • “Each [person’s] path is going to be different. Play to everyone’s strength, and then put them in a position to succeed,” said Matthew Haiken, Retailer Principal at Prestige Collection Auto, during a breakout session at ASOTU CON 2023 titled Getting The Best Out of ALL Your People .

Automotive Headlines: BMW Adopts Tesla Chargers

Giphy

BMW is embracing Tesla's North American Charging Standard for its future electric vehicles, including models under Mini and Rolls-Royce. Starting in 2025, BMW EVs will have built-in compatibility, while existing CCS-outlet vehicles gain Supercharger access, possibly via adapters.

In adopting this charging system, BMW joins multiple automakers, like Ford and Mercedes-Benz, spotlighting Tesla's influential EV infrastructure. 👍🔌🤝

 

A Funny Looking Folding E-Bike

ENGWE/Electrify

Ready to bike up mountains without breaking a sweat? The ENGWE Engine Pro 750W e-bike, priced at $1,399.99, turns heads with its foldable design, 75-mile range, and sleek aesthetics.

Created by passionate riders for versatile terrains, this e-bike promises power and style and boosts health through joint-friendly exercise. 🚴‍♂️🔋💪

 

Volvo Captures The Wind

“A car factory powered by a wind farm” Midjourney/ASOTU

Volvo Cars' Torslanda factory in Sweden is about to get a green boost! Partnering with Eolus Vind, the carmaker's factory and its new battery plant will soon run on electricity from the massive 1 GW Västvind offshore wind farm. This move supports Volvo's ambition to sell only EVs by 2030. 🌬️🚙🔌

 

Stockholm Bans ICE

Starting in 2025, Stockholm will prohibit diesel and gas cars from about 20 blocks of its downtown to combat pollution and prioritize pedestrians and cyclists. Vice Mayor for Transport Lars Strömgren envisions a more breathable, festive city environment.

While other European cities have emission-reducing measures, Stockholm's ban stands distinctively bold. 🇸🇪🚫⛽️

 

Nio Chats Up European Dealers

“Europe and China making a deal to sell more cars” Midjourney/ASOTU

Nio, China's EV giant, is pondering a European dealer network to ramp up sales, as its European performance isn't quite hitting the mark. Despite potential tariffs and competition, Nio remains committed to expansion, considering dealerships for its main brand and the upcoming "Firefly" brand due in 2025. 🚗🌍📈
