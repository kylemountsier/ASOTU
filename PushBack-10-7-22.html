Summer Of Cars at Valley Hi Toyota
 
 
Our friends at Valley Hi Toyota have been busy! Somehow between making brilliant in-house ad spots, they found time to partner with a local radio station for a car giveaway!

And then there was one… The event was attended by over 200 people, with 100 participating as finalists in the giveaway. One after another, their names were called to receive a prize. Eventually, only Rebecca Downes was left as the winner of the Corolla!

Porsche and VW
 

🎩🚀 A launch, a dip, and now a jump have been the short story of Porsche since the launch of its IPO.
 
💶💪 The brand hit 85B euro valuation, officially making it the most valuable carmaker in Europe. A title previously held by its parent Volkswagen.
 
📉😰 The luxury brand dipped to 81B earlier this week but did not stay there long.
 
🇩🇪🇨🇳 Volkswagen is not intimidated by the mix-up and has turned its attention toward a software joint venture in China.
 
💿🚘 According to two sources, neither of which were with VW, the company will invest nearly $1B, but not until the results of an election that will affect two seats on its shareholder supervisory board.
 
Telling it like it is -  We could write a whole email every week just about VW. They are always making moves that, more often than not, seem to work out. They are not the biggest but they are putting in some real work with the leverage they can generate by leaving no option unexplored. We’re taking notes, are yall? 😉

Ford’s F-150 Lightning Gets More Expensive
 
 
This will be the last year Ford produces their half-million dollar GT supercar. People across the country will never adjust to this great loss... Ok, never mind, they are already over it. Let's look at the F-150 Lightning instead.
 
Ford has increased the entry-level cost of the F-150 Lightning by $5000. It is the 2nd price recent price increase as Ford tries to address rising costs stemming from supply chain issues.
 
Since May 2021, the price of the 2023 pro model has increased by 30% - from about $40K to about $52K.
 
Current reservation holders will not be impacted by the price increase. 


Telling it like it is -  We are not mathematically gifted or anything, but it seems super suspect that all these chaotic factors perfectly added up to a cool $5K for customer’s to come up with. 🤓
TECHNOLOGY
Tesla Vision
 

Tesla is scrapping its ultrasonic sensors in favor of "Tesla Vision" cameras. While the sensors currently assist with parking assistance and collision avoidance systems, Tesla has said they would play an essential role in Full Self-Driving.
 
The company will remove the sensors from all its models, temporarily deactivating some assisted features.
 
Current FSD beta testing is being performed using the vision-based camera system that will replace the USS system.
 
Telling it like it is -  Tesla has long been a tech company that sells cars. It will be interesting to see how their abandoning this technology impacts other FSD efforts.
