SEM
Search Engine Marketing
A paid digital marketing strategy that aims to make a website more visible on search engine results pages. Often confused with SEO (search engine optimization), SEM pays for visibility in keyword searches designated by the company spending the ad dollars in an effort to drive clicks to a website landing page. Most typically, SEM dollars are spent via the Google Ads network, with a smaller percentage of spend taking place on Bing.
