Filling Monroe’s Food Pantry

Giphy/Good Mythical Morning

CR Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram of Adrian in southeast Michigan is supporting the Oaks Village Food Pantry in Monroe through a unique initiative.

The food pantry has seen a dramatic increase in demand, giving away more than 2,000 boxes of food in just two months, compared to their previous average of 100 boxes per month. As the school year ends, they are particularly concerned about children going hungry without access to free breakfast and lunch programs.

So, the pantry issued an urgent call for help on social media and the dealership moved quickly to incentivize community involvement. They set up a food drive and organized a contest where donors could win tickets to a Detroit Tigers baseball game.

Driven by compassion and a desire to make a difference, Jessica Liner, a dealer at CR Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram, felt a personal connection to the pantry's plea for assistance. Having witnessed first-hand the hardships faced by others during the pandemic, she wanted to ensure people have access to the food they need. Liner hopes her team’s efforts will inspire others to join in and extend a helping hand.

Telling it like it is – No one is at their best when they’re hungry, but the power of community and compassion can make all the difference for those in need. ❣️

 
AUTOMOTIVE

New Cars Are Cheaper, Leasing EVs is Even Cheaper

Giphy

New vehicle prices in the US remained below the Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) for the fifth consecutive month in May 2023, averaging $48,528. This price is $410 below the sticker price, while a year ago, the average price was $637 above the MSRP. The average transaction price of a new vehicle increased 0.5% month-on-month and 3% year-on-year. Auto manufacturers' incentive spending also rose, averaging $1,914.

Meanwhile, the popularity of leasing battery-electric vehicles (BEVs) has surged, partly due to the introduction of a federal tax credit. Any leased BEV qualifies for the tax credit, which the lessor must pass on to the consumer. The rate of leased BEVs rose from 10% in December 2022 to 41% in April 2023. This trend may increase as BEVs become more affordable, opening the door for those who previously couldn't afford them.

Telling it like it is – It feels like 8 minutes ago leasing was at risk due to shifting prices. Now this. It all just proves change is the only constant, y’all.

A New Beatles Song?

Giphy

In a surprising twist that will make even the most die-hard Beatles fan cheer, Paul McCartney has revealed that he's harnessed the power of generative AI to bring his band back together for one last hurrah.

The legendary bassist spilled the beans during a recent interview with BBC on how he used AI to recreate the voices of his late bandmates, John Lennon and George Harrison, as well as making his own voice sound younger. The technology was used to expertly “extricate" Lennon's voice from a demo track that was featured in the 2021 documentary, “ The Beatles: Get Back.”

Referring to the AI track as the “final” song from the Beatles, McCartney said he plans to release it later this year.

Telling it like it is – The Beatles continue to transcend time, proving that even in the digital age, all you need is love (and, maybe a little computational magic). 🤖
