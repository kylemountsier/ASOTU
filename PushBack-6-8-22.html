"Touch A Truck" At Byers Automotive
 
 
Jimmy Jones and the Byers Automotive group continue to hold community engagement at the foundation of their culture. A recent "Touch a Truck" event at the dealership saw some 3000 people meet 50 first responders. The first responders brought 30 vehicles and pieces of equipment for the guests to see up close and, of course, touch.
 
Joining the event were over 30 private company vendors who brought their vehicles and equipment for the community to check out. 
 
In addition to welcoming people to a day of fun, the folks at Byers collected several thousand canned goods for the local Grove City Food Pantry!
