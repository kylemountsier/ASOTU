Crossfitters to Combat Fuel Prices: US Department of Energy
 
 
Gas prices are sooo high... no punchline is coming. They're just high! In the spirit of innovation, one Troublemaking Dealer saw a problem and a solution right before our eyes.
 
Fitness is my life! Patrick Abad, GM of Beaver Toyota in Cumming, Georgia, is no stranger to fitness. Patrick oversees an impressive 135,000 square foot facility containing a showroom, service center, entertainment area, cafe, and, most important for our story: a fitness center.
 
When the US Department of Energy came knocking, Patrick box-jumped into action. Shifting attention to training, actual physical training, Patrick's store became the home base of the PUSHBACK FITNESS app (Always in all caps because of yelling), on which fuel price-fatigued users can actually rent Crossfitters to combat fuel prices.
 
Off to a strong start. "It's really simple, actually," Abad said, "you enter your location in the app, and within minutes an outrageously hype guy or gal shows up and pushes your car up hills!" Once a car reaches the top of the hill, the Crossfitter gets in to coast down before getting out at the bottom of the next hill.
 
The US Department of Energy reports a significant reduction in fuel consumption since the program launched 4 hours ago.
 
Telling it like it is - Chicken prices are also on the rise, which means the cost of feeding crossfitters could close the gap on fuel prices, losing those precious gains.

David Long’s Positivity Tests ‘Negative’?
 
 
People around the ASOTU-verse are familiar with our friend, industry leader, and ASOTU investor David Long. His Pandemic of Positivity movement has been a big part of the industry's drive to band together and push back. But what is this "Positivity," and how contagious is it?
 
ASOTU is on the case. We dove into the question, and our findings were scandalous, to say the least. There were no known cases of positivity infections before David Long. We consulted the only known positivity test, the Strengthsfinder Assessment, and while positivity can be detected, no evidence of its contagiousness has been confirmed. Is Long patient zero? Or is this whole pandemic a delusion? 
 
Friends and colleagues alike have called on David to silence these suspicions by releasing test results showing conclusive evidence of this previously unheard-of phantom. As of today, April 1, David has not released any proof or statement.

Exclusive interview With Viral Automotive News Knee.
 
 
Some people meet their heroes at NADA. Leaders and innovators from the automotive industry rubbing elbows and standing shoulder to shoulder with newcomers. It is rare but not impossible for such heroes to be born at NADA. One Knee defied the odds and became an overnight sensation, household name, and viral internet trend.
 
We caught up with The Knee on a chilly NY morning to bend back some of the complex layers of America's most beloved celebrity.
 
Thanks for being so flexible with your time. Can you walk us through a day in your life?
 
That's literally the main thing I do.
 
Tell us how you prepared for your role?
 
Since going viral, I'm me-deep in reporters and tweets and people trying to get me to do interviews like this one. Nothing I could have done would have prepared me for all of this. I was in the gym, focused on the lower thigh/upper calves, waiting for my moment but I should have been hiring an agent... or joint specialist.
 
While everybody's eyes are on you, who do you look up to?
 
Well, by default, I look up at pretty much everybody.
 
Oh yeah, sorry, we mean, who inspires you?
 
From a young age, I would watch WWE's The People's Elbow and knew who I wanted to be. Who remembers the guy that elbow was attached to? Nobody. Everybody remembers that powerful elbow. Just like they'll remember me.
 
Before we go, we have to ask, can you tell us about the #DontBeKneedy movement?
 
Knees everywhere deserve more recognition. Imagine a day without them? Horrible right? So Don't Be Kneedy is all about recognizing the knees that keep folks going. It's my way of using my platform for good. 
 
Conclusion: Shout out to The Knee for its time and also make sure you thank Automotive News Publisher KC Krain and writer C.J. Moore for introducing the nation to the #DontBeKneedy movement. To join the movement, simply post an image of your knee on social media and tag ASOTU.

From Successful B&B Owner to Self-made Astronaut
 
 
Everybody knows the most challenging place to sell a car is in the middle of an active volcano. B&B owner Brian Kramer is aiming just short of that in collaboration with Axiom Space. Orbit Toyota will launch in early April 2024 and feature a 36,000 square foot showroom, just a bit bigger than the International Space Station.
 
Kramer is also partnering with Blue Origin to get customers to his high-flying dealership. They will ride a New Shephard launch vehicle to Orbit Toyota, dock, and be able to shop. After making a purchase, customers will re-enter Earth's atmosphere in their new car, strapped onto a heat shield. After landing safely, the heat shield will automatically disconnect, allowing owners to begin enjoying their brand new Toyota.
 
When we asked Axiom Space liaison Venus Armstrong for a statement, she said, "Who are you, and how did you get in my car?"
 
Can Kramer be stopped? Kramer recently trademarked Kra-moon-er and Kra-mars, so one can only imagine what his following ambitions will be.
