MORE THAN CARS MOMENTS

Adorning Our Heroes

Giphy

Serving a community near Fort Cavazos, Cleo Bay Subaru of Killeen fully  understands the importance of supporting our men and women in uniform. 🎖️

They recently donated a substantial $16K to Wreaths for Vets , a nonprofit dedicated to honoring fallen soldiers. The contribution will not only fund new festoons for the more than 14,500 heroes resting at the Central Texas State Veterans Cemetery but also create a facility on the property to store all the wreaths.

Fort Cavazos Herald / Wreaths for Vets

“It only makes sense to pair up with a veterans charity,” said Sales Manager Jon Locklin. “It helps most of our population here — retired, active, spouse, family.”

Their benevolence doesn’t stop there, because Cleo Bay Subaru of Killeen has a heart as big as the Lone Star State. Thanks to their yearly Share the Love event, they have given more than $177K to local nonprofits since they opened their doors. Other organizations that have been blessed with their generosity include the Fort Hood Area Habitat for Humanity, Texas Humane Heroes, Boys and Girls Club of Central Texas, and Guardian Angels for Soldier’s Pet. ❤️‍🔥

 
DATA & INSIGHT

Trucks VS Avg Monthly Payments

Giphy/HORNBACH

Car prices have surged, and consumers are feeling the pinch. Edmunds.com data reveals a shocking $733 average monthly payment for new cars. Used cars are just a little behind at $571. Let's understand why and how we can help our customers navigate this market.

🚗   Why the Surge?   Pickup truck purchases , especially in states like Texas and Wyoming. These once-utilitarian vehicles have become status symbols, particularly in regions of rugged work like farming and energy production.

🤝   The   Dealer Role : Customers trust their local dealer for guidance. So when panic, anxiety, and stress seem to keep people from coming in or settling on a purchase

  • Help them focus on the specifics of their decision

  • Emphasize the actual value of the vehicle

  • Showcase financing options

  • Understand and cater to their needs where possible

Though car prices have shifted, we have the opportunity to ensure our customers feel our vehicles' unparalleled value and utility.

🛣️ The Road Ahead As you know, cars aren't just numbers. They're about experiences. Let's give customers confidence about their journey by showing they aren't traveling alone.

Tesla's Battery Longevity

Midjourney/ASOTU

  • Recurrent   studied   12,500 US Tesla vehicles over five years.

  • Found no significant range degradation difference between frequently fast-charged EVs and those rarely fast-charged.

  • Suggests Tesla's battery management system effectively protects during routine fast charging.

  • 🚨 Note: Fast charging in extreme conditions might increase battery risk.

📈 European Car Sales Growth

Midjourney/ASOTU

  • July 2023 : European car sales up by 15.2% , twelve consecutive months of growth .

  • EV Sales: Up 60.6% , accounting for 13.6% of total sales.

  • Despite the surge in EVs, petrol and diesel cars still lead the market but are declining.

Toyota's System Glitch 🛠️

Midjourney/ASOTU

  • Malfunction: During a system update , all Toyota Motor plants in Japan stopped. 🛑

  • Potential loss: $356 million in revenue.

  • July 2023 Sales: Up by 8% globally.

  • Projected Production for the year: A record 10.2M vehicles .

  • Official word: The malfunction was not due to a cyber attack.

Electrogenic's Classic Conversion 🚗⚡

Midjourney/ASOTU

  • Electrogenic unveils the conversion of a 1929 Rolls-Royce Phantom II into an EV.

  • Features a 93 kWh battery pack with up to 150 miles of range.

  • Preserves the vehicle's vintage aesthetics while providing modern EV tech.

🍔 A Whopper of a Lawsuit

Giphy/Nick

A U.S. District Judge in Miami rejected Burger King's motion to shrug off a sizzling lawsuit. The bone of contention? The menu board visuals might give the Whopper sandwich a bit more "whop" than reality.

🍖   Meaty Details:

  • Accusation : The fast-food titan is said to portray a Whopper that's a whopping 35% larger and double the meat serving.

  • Burger King's Defense : "Our advertisements are accurate!" But Judge Altman thinks the jury should have the final bite.

Burger King isn't dining solo on this controversial table. McDonald's, Wendy's, and Taco Bell are all simmering in similar lawsuits, each dishing out claims for a juicy $5M in damages.
