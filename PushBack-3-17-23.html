Mississippi Bill Guides EV Sales

Giphy/Rebellion Developments

Gov. Tate Reeves of Mississippi has signed a bill requiring EV manufacturers open franchise dealerships if they want to sell to customers in person. A pro at dotting the "i"s, the Gov. signed the bill despite calls from fellow Republicans to veto the measure.

"Over 200 small businesses in communities across our state are seeking assurance that big manufacturers can't just destroy their business. That's fair!" Reeves said. He acknowledged that innovation is inevitable but is committed to finding a long-term solution as the market changes. 

An opponent to the bill said the state cannot afford to lose, and anybody who refuses to innovate will lose out.

VW will release its first affordable EV in 2025. The ID. 2all will cost around $27,000, have a 280-mile range, and feature some now-common beeps and whistles like a route planner/travel assistant.

Plans on Plans. Since the company announced its $200B 5-year electrification plan, all its announcements seem like background noise in a sci-fi movie. Giphy/Katie Price/ASOTU

Audi has affordability plans of its own. Now that chips are getting easier to find, they figure, "Why not put some chips in cheaper cars?" Which is the exact same question most people ask before they get ants. 

Last year the brand saw earnings climb 40% as it prioritized higher-price models. While revenue will climb with 2023's product mix normalization, earnings expectations are more conservative since higher costs will weigh on profits. 

CEO Markus Duesmann said the company has its sights on 100% electrification with EV offerings in its core segments by 2027 . He did not share details but said the company is working on an entry-level EV to replace the A3.

Giphy/Disney

Manufacturers are working fast to adopt renewable sources like sun, water, wind, or organic materials to generate green energy in response to the increasing global energy demand and a greater focus on a net-zero emissions future.

United Airlines is partnering with biotechnology company Viridos to turn algae into jet fuel for their commercial fleets. UA has invested $5M to create sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) as part of its commitment to eliminate all of its greenhouse gas emissions by 2050. The company expects the fuel to have a 70% reduced carbon footprint compared to traditional fuel.

Giphy/HBO

The European Union is considering plans to offer subsidies for hydrogen production using renewable energy sources such as wind and solar. The proposed subsidies aim to make clean hydrogen more competitive and support the EU's goal of becoming climate-neutral by 2050. 

Giphy/Lucasfilm

Emissions-free green  hydrogen could soon be an important addition to the range of clean energies. Green hydrogen uses electricity from renewable energy sources to separate hydrogen from water in a process known as electrolysis . The element is not a primary energy source in the same way that fossil fuels are, but rather an energy carrier like electricity.

Though green hydrogen currently faces immutable laws of chemistry, physics, and logistics that will require major technological breakthroughs before it can become scalable, developers are up for the challenge.
