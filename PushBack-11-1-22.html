Mazda Setting Up for 2030 Push
 
 
Mazda began catching up to the rest of the market with a 43% production increase in September and an announcement of a new brand purpose.
 
The new brand purpose is expected to include a new EV strategy and roadmap to 2030 amidst Mazda’s rollout of the new CX-70 and CX-90 in the U.S. and the CX-60 and CX-80 internationally.
 
Telling it like it is - Somewhere along the way we were hopeful every OEM on the planet wasn’t preparing their 2030 EV strategy…our hopes are quickly waning.

Drop It Like Its a Used Car
 
 
Tell us something we didn’t know. It is hitting the airwaves that more than just the auto industry knows prices on used cars are dropping. With public companies like Carmax and AutoNation reporting sales volume declines in Q3, the fourth quarter has been home to a steadily dropping used car pricing trend.
 
Cox Automotive reported that new vehicle availability topped 1.4 million, up from its 1 million mark for the majority of the year. This, along with steep interest rate hikes have contributed to slowing showroom traffic and lower used car prices in many places across the country.
 
Telling it like it is - Don’t freak out. We still have story after story of growth last month in the hopper. Find the market share that is out there for the taking, even in market shifts like this.

Panel for Policy
 
 
In May, Musk promised to reverse the permanent ban on Trump’s Twitter account if Musk were to purchase Twitter, stating that he did not agree with the permanent ban policy.
 
After officially buying the social media platform, Musk has now stated that he will be implementing a panel to review all suspended/banned accounts on Twitter.
 
Telling it like it is - What are the odds this new review panel of Musk’s will decide to reverse Trump’s Twitter ban just in time for the 2024 Presidential campaigning season?
