Hey, check this out!
 
Our good friend Brian Kramer, GM for Accu-Trade, recently ran a little experiment in Naples, Florida to see how people would react when he tried to appraise and buy their cars right off the street. EV Skateboards
 
 
⏰What are "EV Skateboards?" 
 
🛹EV Skateboards have been around since 2002, when GM first debuted them. Originally they were designed for hydrogen fuel cell technology.
 
🤓They are a chassis system that makes EVs viable, which has led to a proliferation of EV start-ups.
 
⛽The Skateboard is essentially the 3rd chassis type in the last 60 years. The new style is better for EVs because the other two options were created to house systems essential for ICE but unneeded in an EV.
 
Telling it like it is — We are sharing this information because with better energy efficiency and a road-hugging low center of gravity, this could be the foundation of cars for the next 6o years.

Stellantis Buys Back Stock
 
 
Stellantis is buying back some of itself from GM. A $920M deal between the companies will return 69.1 million common shares or 2.2% of the company's share capital to Stellantis.
 
Sweeten the deal. In addition to the cash payment, GM will receive 1.2 million shares of part maker Faurecia SE and about 130 million euros for rights to dividends paid by Peugeot and Stellantis. 
 
Telling it like it is — This feels like a really expensive way to tell somebody you aren't friends anymore.
