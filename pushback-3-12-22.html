Time Dealer of the Year 2022 Announced
 
 
Each year The Time Dealer of the Year award celebrates exceptional performance and outstanding community service in the industry. 

Out of a packed list of nominees, Bob Giles stood above the rest!

Bob Is the CEO of Giles Automotive Inc., a 3rd generation dealer, and started at the store when he was just eleven years old. His 92-year-old father was in the audience to see his son accept the award.
 
Telling it like it is -  Bob shared a quote to close his speech and we agree it is worth sharing. "The things we have are the evidence of great living, but the only things that we give are the evidence of a great life." - Winston Churchill

A Double Dip of All Things Used Cars
 
 
David Long Hosted Back-To-Back Episodes of "All Things Used Cars." One of Which Was Livestreamed From The NADA Show Floor! The first conversation was on fire with the excitement of NADA starting later in the morning. He continued to bring awareness back to one question: How do we know which cars are the right ones to buy? 

If the first session was a fire the second was a volcano! David was live from the NADA floor. After spending time speaking with Dale Pollak about his new book, Whole Truth, he invited folks to share their experience and expertise with him. We did our best to capture some of the rapid-fire gems shared by all. 
 
Telling it like it is - If you know David Long (and we do), you know when he brings a crowd turning on the mics is the only option!

Google Debuts Auto Ad Search
 
 

Google is launching a platform that allows dealerships to place ads in relevant search results. The ads will appear in related searches displaying photos, price, mileage, and location. 

Isn't that already what Google does? The tool aims to help local dealers get their inventory online and visible to interested parties. Google said advertisers who tried the vehicle ads service experienced a 25% increase in meaningful engagements.
 
Telling it like it is - If social media can make a first impression, Google can be used to window shop.
