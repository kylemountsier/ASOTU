Toyota Cuts Uber Stake
 
 
Stemming from Uber's decision to halt autonomous driving research, Toyota has cut its 10.25m shares in the company's technology arm by half. Toyota wants to maintain a collaborative relationship with Uber but continues working toward self-driving tech with Aurora Innovation, the company that bought Uber's Autonomous driving unit.
 
Telling it like it is —  Toyota gave Uber the “lets still be friends” treatment while slowly selling 5 million pieces of the friendship bracelet they made together…

BMW Begins Production At New Plant
 
 
A $2.2B BMW plant in China has started producing EVs. It is the company's third plant in China and will increase annual regional output by 130K vehicles.
 
The plant will begin by producing the pure electric i3 midsize sports sedan but is equipped to flexibly produce other EV models.
 
Fact attack —  China accounts for 57.4% of global EV production and 53% of “new energy” passenger vehicles being driven.

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
META Brings Two New Worlds To Cannes
 
 
Every year the Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity brings the creative communication industry together for learning, networking, and celebrating. This festival is META's first year returning after a two-year covid break. They brought a sneak peek into the Metaverse's future...well, actually, they brought two.
 
MINIVerse is the Meta Creative Shop's first collaboration with an automotive group. The BMW Group brand, MINI, worked to reimagine go-karting in Meta Horizon Worlds. Players can customize their karts and race others on high-speed and high-flying tracks.
 
The Fender Stratosphere Horizon World is a floating guitar-shaped island featuring the first of its kind co-play original music creation. The Meta Fender collaboration allows visitors to participate in a scavenger hunt for musical elements they can use to create interesting music with other visitors. 
 
Each collaboration shares a quality of inviting people to enjoy the brand's creativity with or without becoming traditional customers. An invitation to become something more than a consumer, but a collaborator in these company's early ventures into what the Metaverse can mean for brand relationships.
