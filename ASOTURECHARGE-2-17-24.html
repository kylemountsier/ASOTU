Built LEGO Tough

Lego / CarScoops

Legoland Florida is about to drop something that’s part LEGO, part Ferrari, and 100% awesome.

Their new Lego Ferrari Build & Race Experience will open on March 8th, and attendees can create their own Ferrari from LEGO and race it against others in a special “virtual race zone.”

Central to the launch is a life-size Ferrari 296 GTS, meticulously assembled from LEGO bricks by a team of 10 over 1,850 hours.

This build isn't all show, either. It features working lights, real Ferrari wheels, and a level of detail that blurs the line between toy and automobile.

Curious about the brick count? So are we, but they’re keeping that one tight to the chest for now. But you can head over to their IG to give your best guess. 🧮
