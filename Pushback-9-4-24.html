VW
Volkswagen is considering closing two German plants due to a significant 15% drop in EV sales across Europe and the U.S., signaling potential disruptions in the supply chain.

Rivian
Meanwhile, Rivian continues to push forward despite a recent fire at its Illinois factory that destroyed over 50 EVs—production remains steady, backed by $800 million in state funding for expansion.

Polestar
Lastly, Polestar appointed a new CFO, Jean-Francois Mady, as part of its ongoing executive reshuffle, just as the company secured $300 million in additional funding to meet its $1.3 billion target.

Quick Hits: Entertainment, Economy, and Science

  • Sony is taking its latest PlayStation game, Concord , offline on September 6th due to poor sales and low player engagement, offering full refunds to all purchasers as they explore future options, potentially including a shift to a free-to-play model. 💾 

  • African countries are losing up to 5% of their GDP annually due to climate change, with the agriculture sector suffering a 34% decline in productivity since 1961. Despite facing severe impacts, these nations receive only 20% of global climate adaptation funding, far below what is needed to build resilience and protect their economies and populations. 🌽
