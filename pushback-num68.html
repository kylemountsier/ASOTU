Bitcoin Drops Below $40k For First Time In 6 Months
 
 

A late afternoon dip in the stock market January 20th saw a correlative dip in the value of Bitcoin the following day. Research Director Clara Medalie cites this trend as evidence Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies are no longer isolated assets but are becoming more entwined with the market. He goes on to point out Bitcoin and other cryptos are often drawn on in times of margin calls, so when the market dips Bitcoin is at risk of sell-off as well dropping confidence in the stock and therefore dipping its value.
