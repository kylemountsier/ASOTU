GM Raises Hope Of Increased Chip Supply
 
 

GM noted that the outlook is up on chip supply, but still is not optimistic about inventory levels returning to ‘normal’ levels. For dealers, a better flow of inventory certainly means higher projections on volume, as well as the hope that profits remain high amidst continued lack of full supply.

Following suit of other manufacturers, Daimler and Mercedes-Benz are throwing on the boxing gloves to fight for the ‘forefront’ of EV development. The OEM also has its eyes set on direct to consumer sales models with the new emphasis on electrification, digitalisation and autonomous driving.
