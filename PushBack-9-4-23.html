All Roads Lead to Greenfield with Steve Greenfield - Auto Collabs

CEO and Founder of Automotive Ventures, a visionary in automotive technology and mobility. With roles at TrueCar, AutoTrader, and Manheim, Steve's profound impact on the industry showcases significant acquisitions and global ventures.

 

Darren Doane

Hailed by MTV's Matt Pinfield as "the most influential director in the alternative, punk rock scene." Defining voice of a generation, Darren has shaped iconic bands like Blink 182 and AFI. A maestro in music videos, his portfolio spans action flicks to indies, breaking categories.

Auto Throws a Punch with Darren Doane

 

Patrice Banks

Trailblazer in the automotive industry, empowering countless women to become confident #SHECANICS. Engineer turned auto expert, her initiative, Girls Auto Clinic, stands acclaimed nationwide. TEDx speaker, author, and now collaborating with Warner Bros, Patrice doesn’t just break barriers; she redefines them.

 

Colby Joyner

Hailing from Victoria, Texas, this Automotive News 40 under 40 nominee boasts a diverse portfolio spanning retail automotive, tech/software, and seamless software integration. As the former Platform Director for Bravo Auto Group, Colby's leadership, and fervor for uplifting those around him shine brightly. Beyond the workspace, he cherishes moments with his wife, three kids, and friends.

 

Damon Lester

From CPA specializing in nonprofits to leading NAMAD's mission, Damon's journey reveals his dedication to creating equitable opportunities in the automotive industry. Under his leadership, NAMAD has evolved into an influential voice in legislation, empowered ~300 minority entrepreneurs, and formed strategic alliances with global auto manufacturers, championing diversity and inclusion.

 

David Long

Executive General Manager of Hansel Auto Group, David's vast experience spans roles as a GM, owner, consultant, and more in the auto realm. A Clubhouse host for "All Things Used Cars," he's also the visionary behind the 'Pandemic of Positivity.’

 

Erin Sparks

As Marketing Director for the DeNooyer Automotive Family, Erin boasts 15 years of Michigan-centric marketing expertise. Tasked with managing websites, analytics, advertising, and outreach, she excels in people management and fine-tuning processes. A Wisconsin native and ex-college volleyball player, Erin cherishes moments with her family and is a passionate event planner.

Reshaping Lives

Vehicles for Change/ASOTU

Our ASOTU CON 2023 charity partner, Vehicles for Change, is about more than cars in a big way. They’re reshaping lives and the industry through transformative training, support, and employment. 🙌

By championing those re-entering society post-incarceration, Vehicles for Change paves the path to reinvigorated families, decreased recidivism, and new opportunities.
