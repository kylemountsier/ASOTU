Gates Automotive and Relay for Life
 
 
Our friends at Gates Automotive partnered with their local Relay for Life in a unique way!
 
The cancer research fundraising organization's St. Joseph County group hosted an event in June that Gates Automotive supported financially and with their time and skills.
 
By contributing media coverage for the event, Gates Automotive helped RFL's opportunity to give reach even more people.
 
The event in June collected $10K, while for 2022, the St. Joseph County group raised $100K total!
 
 
Gate's own Judy Morgan is on the leadership team of RFL and helped put together a press release highlighting the fun and importance of the 24-hour event.
 
It shows that these groups often need more than money to leave their mark on our communities. They need our time and talent.

Virtual Cars and Electric Deliveries
 
 
Renault and Google have upped their ambitions vehicle software goals. The 4-year-old partnership has been working toward unique software enhancements but now says the goal is "software-defined vehicles."
 
What does that even mean? Well, the two companies will work together to create a simulated vehicle clone for testing. They will test vehicles, software, and AI features in the simulation to diagnose and improve upon issues before a "real" vehicle ever leaves the factory.
